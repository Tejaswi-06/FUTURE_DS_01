# 📊 Data Analytics Dashboard Project

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project showcases three interactive dashboards built using **Power BI** to analyze different business domains:

💹 Business Sales Performance Analysis  
🔄 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis  
📢 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis  

The goal of this project is to extract meaningful insights, track KPIs, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 💹 1. Business Sales Performance Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze overall business sales performance and identify trends across products, regions, and payment methods.

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Sales: 125.59M  
* Total Profit: 21.99M  
* Average Order Value: 125.59K  
* Profit Margin: 17.51%  

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Top products like Mini Stapler and Ring Binder drive highest sales**
* Sales peaked in **2024 and dropped significantly in 2025**
* **UPI and Card payments contribute major share**
* Certain regions dominate overall revenue contribution

## 📊 Visualizations Used

* Bar Chart (Sales by Product)  
* Line Chart (Sales & Growth by Year)  
* Map (Sales by Region)  
* Stacked Bar (Sales by Category)  
* Donut Chart (Sales by Payment Type)  
* Gauge Chart (Profit Margin)  
* Slicers (Product, Region, Category, Payment Method)

# 🔄 2. Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze customer retention patterns and identify key factors contributing to churn.

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Customers: 5K  
* Churned Customers: 2K  
* Active Customers: 3K  
* Average Tenure: 36.10  
* Monthly Charges: 4.30M  

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn**
* Customers using **Fiber internet show higher churn**
* **Basic subscription users churn more than premium**
* Churn trend shows a **sharp drop in recent year**

## 📊 Visualizations Used

* Bar Chart (Active Customers by Year)  
* Line Chart (Churn Trend)  
* Pie Chart (Churn by Gender & Subscription)  
* Area Chart (Internet Service & Churn)  
* Bar Chart (Churn Rate by Contract)  
* KPI Cards  
* Slicers (Gender, Contract Type, Subscription, Payment Method)

# 📢 3. Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze the marketing funnel and evaluate conversion performance across channels and campaigns.

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Impressions: 28M  
* Total Clicks: 2M  
* Total Leads: 570K  
* Total Customers: 92K  
* CTR: 0.07  
* CAC: 172.92  

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Major drop-off occurs between Leads and Customers**
* **Instagram and Email generate higher customer conversions**
* Campaign performance varies across types
* Marketing spend is not always directly proportional to leads

## 📊 Visualizations Used

* Funnel Chart (Funnel Stage Performance)  
* Bar Chart (Customers by Channel)  
* Line Chart (Customers by Month)  
* Donut Chart (Spend by Channel)  
* Combo Chart (Leads vs Spend)  
* Slicers (Channel, Campaign, Date)
