## 📝 Problem Statement
Businesses generate a large amount of sales data, but analyzing raw data is difficult and time-consuming.  
The main challenge is to track overall business performance and identify key trends such as sales growth, profit, customer spending, and region-wise contribution.

This project aims to build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that provides a clear and detailed view of sales performance using KPIs and visual reports.  
The dashboard helps stakeholders make better decisions by quickly identifying high-performing products, categories, payment methods, and regions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- To analyze **Total Sales** and **Total Profit**
- To track **Sales Growth by Year**
- To identify **Top-Selling Products**
- To analyze sales performance by **Region**
- To compare sales by **Category and Year**
- To understand sales contribution by **Payment Method**
- To monitor overall **Profit Margin Performance**

---

## 📌 Key KPIs
- **Total Sales**
- **Total Profit**
- **Average Order Value**
- **Profit Margin %**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
### Visuals Included:
- Total Sales by Product (Bar Chart)
- Sales & Growth by Year (Line Chart)
- Total Sales by Region (Map)
- Sales by Year & Category (Stacked Column Chart)
- Sales by Payment Type (Donut Chart)
- Profit Margin Performance (Gauge Chart)

---

## 🎛 Filters / Slicers
- Payment Method  
- Product  
- Region  
- Category
